About Africa Education Holdings

Africa Education Holdings is a leading provider of educational resources and services for students, teachers, and institutions across the continent. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, we strive to empower individuals and communities through accessible and high-quality educational offerings.

AEH owns and operates four private universities in Uganda and Zambia (Anglophone) and Senegal and Mali (Francophone) with a combined Total Enrolment of 14,500 students.

AEH is passionate about serving its university partners, the students and alumni of those universities, their faculty and their staff. We also aim to serve the communities and countries in which the universities operate. We believe that through these partnerships we can transform Africa into an economic and intellectual powerhouse. This feat is based on the strength and knowledge of her people, her sheer size and scale and her vastly untapped resources which all Africans deserve to share.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, market-relevant, and transformative education to Africa’s youth. We are pioneering a new model of an African university designed to prepare and help each student achieve gainful employment and entrepreneurship on a global scale, unlocking Africa’s vast economic potential.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every young African has the opportunity to attain a world-class education that empowers them to become ethical leaders, innovators, and catalysts for sustainable development within their communities and across the globe.

Our Investors

FinnFund

Finnfund is a Finnish Development Finance Institution (DFI) which builds a sustainable world by investing in responsible and profitable businesses that drive sustainable developments in developing countries.

FinnFund provides businesses operating in developing countries with risk capital, long-term investment loans, mezzanine financing and expertise on how to invest in the developing markets. FinnFund has invested approximately EUR 557 million primarily in clean energy, sustainable forestry, financial institutions, and agriculture. Maarifa is Finnfund’s first investment in the education sector.
